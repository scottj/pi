Other approaches

  • Leibniz series — easy to teach; very slow to converge.
  • Nilakantha series — still simple, noticeably faster convergence.
  • Chudnovsky — the standard choice for huge digit counts.
  • Monte Carlo — good for intuition and demos, not exact digits.
