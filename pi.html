What this version does

The page keeps the same core math as the CLI: integer scaling, guard digits for rounding, and a Machin-style identity built from arctangent series.

Formula
π / 4 = 4 arctan(1 / 5) - arctan(1 / 239)
Approach
Fixed-point arithmetic with BigInt

Other ways to estimate pi

  • Leibniz series: easy to teach, but very slow to converge.
  • Nilakantha series: still simple, with a noticeably better convergence rate.
  • Chudnovsky: the usual choice when you need huge digit counts.
  • Monte Carlo: useful for demos and intuition, not exact decimal output.
